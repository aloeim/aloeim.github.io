Privacy Policy

Home Privacy Policy

Chia Inc. (hereinafter “Chia”) has set forth the policy on the treatment of personal information regarding the personal information handled in the management of mining business and mining equipment rental business, with this policy as well as the applicable laws and regulations to ensure thorough compliance.
1. Acquisition of Personal Information
Chia will specify the purposes of use of Personal Information, and acquire Personal Information in the necessary scope and appropriate manner.
2. Purposes of Use of Personal Information
Chia will use Personal Information only within the scope of the purposes stated below unless separately notified or announced, or except otherwise approved by law or ordinances.
About information acquired in connection with the services provided by Chia
* Information about services
* Responding to various inquiries
* Procedure for authentication of services and procedure of invoice etc.
* Other, the purpose accompanying the above
3. Management of Personal Data
Chia will take all measures that are necessary and appropriate to prevent disclosure, loss, impairment and any other mistreatment of Personal Data. In order to ensure the safe management of Personal Data, Chia will supervise its employee as necessary and appropriate. When outsourcing the handling of Personal Data, all or in part, Chia will supervise all outsourced service providers as necessary and appropriate.
4. Provision of Personal Data to Third Parties
Chia will not provide Personal Data to any third parties without the prior consent of the principal identified by the relevant Personal Data or unless authorized or approved by law or ordinances.
5. Joint Use of Personal Data
As far as the purpose of use described in the above "2. Purpose of Use of Personal Information", there may be cases that companies of Chia jointly use Personal Data, such as names, addresses, phone numbers, e-mail addresses and all other items.
6. Request for Notification of Purposes of Use, Disclosure, Correction, Discontinuation of Use or other Treatment of Personal Data
With respect to any Personal Data held by Chia, if the principal identified requests any of the items below, Chia will verify that the request is actually being made by or on behalf of the principal, Chia will respond to the request in accordance with the applicable laws.
* Notification of the purpose of use of the Personal Data;
* Disclosure to the principal of the relevant Personal Data that enable identification of the principal;
* Correction or deletion of, or addition to, the relevant Personal Data that enable identification of the principal; or
* Discontinuation of use, erasure or discontinuation of the provision to third parties of the relevant Personal Data.
7. Inquiries
In the case of any inquiry regarding this Privacy Policy or any treatment thereunder, please contact the following: info@chi.as
8. Modification of this Personal Information Protection Policy
Chia may modify this Privacy Policy as necessary, and any such modification will be posted on this website.
